The Architecture of CertVault

Ventarc CertVault utilizes **elliptical curve cryptography (ECC)** to generate immutable digital credentials. Every certificate issued is uniquely hashed and timestamped, creating a verifiable link between the attendee and the event authority.

  • check_circle Zero-Knowledge Proofs: Verify credentials without exposing sensitive attendee data.
  • check_circle Anti-Tamper Layer: Cryptographic seals that break if any pixel of the PDF is altered.
  • check_circle Permanent Storage: Decentralized storage options ensuring access for years.
